Hey! Welcome to my website. My name is Alex, and I am an ambitious software developer looking to grow my technical and personal skills at a company where my key graphic design skills and familiarity with software development can be applied to help the organization reach its goals. I am proficient in producing and editing computer graphics through Adobe PhotoShop, creating logos in Adobe Illustrator, and can create interactive prototypes of iOS/Android and web applications using Adobe XD.

I started my journey in software development back in High School. The story doesn't start off with me learning a programming language, but instead learning how to swap the heads of different animals onto other animals and sometimes myself. My tech professor, Mr. G thought it would be a good thing if I picked up using Adobe Photoshop as a hobby, and so I did. I spent the whole summer before my first semester of college working with Photoshop. Creating all types of hot garbage, but after the summer was over I actually became pretty decent with all the tools. I began creating logos and soon enough, mock ups for mobile games I wanted to create.
